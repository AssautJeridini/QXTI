# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want QXTI to be a welcoming, respectful, and technically serious collaboration space.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and discuss disagreements in good faith.
- Focus critique on ideas, code, and evidence.
- Help keep discussions clear, reproducible, and useful for others.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or exclusionary behavior.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.
- Sharing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, moderate, or remove contributions that violate these expectations and may restrict participation when necessary to protect the project environment.
